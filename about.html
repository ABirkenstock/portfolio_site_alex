Welcome to my “About” page

Hello again, I’m Alex, a multidisciplinary and award-winning creative making a transition to product design. With a passion for problem-solving and storytelling, I am committed to crafting exceptional experiences that leave a lasting impact. Having just completed an intensive 500-hour training program in UX Design, I am now looking for a job in which I can expand my skills.

Some things about me:

  • Design Thinking aficionado: I started using this method when studying Communication design, fell in love with the results and have been using it in all design projects since.
  • Natural storyteller: I’ve created an entire fictional universe when I was 7 to captivate my peers with thrilling narratives. This skill is really handy when translating complex systems into easy-to-consume user stories (and to communicate my designs to other designers, stakeholders, and clients).

  • Can make people feel comfortable in an interview: I’ve had plenty of experience interviewing people during my time as a content producer.
  • Organized: I can’t make a point here, I just don’t like clutter.

But talking more technical here,
my key skills in the UX field include:

  • Proficiency in persona creation and user-centric design strategies.
  • Project management expertise to ensure seamless execution and delivery.
  • Strong graphic design capabilities and expertise in design language systems.
  • Proficient in the Adobe Suite (XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), Miro, and Notion. - Quick adaptability to new software platforms.
  • Basic HTML, CSS, and webflow development skills.
